The International Yoga Day is celebrated every year on June 21. Yoga is India's gift to humanity and it is a holistic approach to health and well-being, balancing mind, body and soul, President Ram Nath Kovind said on June 21, greeting people on the International Yoga Day.
In a bid to spread awareness about the importance and hoistic health benefits of Yoga, 21st June is celebrated as the International Yoga Day every year since 2015. The ancient practice of Yoga that has stemmed from Indian culture, is widely known to balance body and mind of an individual.
